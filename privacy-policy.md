## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Location Data

The App collects your device's **precise or approximate geographic location** to provide core tourism and travel features, such as:

- Displaying nearby attractions, landmarks, and points of interest
- Providing navigation and directional guidance
- Personalising content based on your current location

Location data is collected **only while you are actively using the App** (foreground access). We do not collect location data in the background without your explicit consent.

You may revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings. Note that disabling location access may limit core App functionality.

### 3.2 Crash and Diagnostic Data

We use a crash reporting service (such as Crashlytics or Sentry) that automatically collects diagnostic information when the App encounters an error. This may include:

- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Stack traces and error logs
- General device state at the time of the crash (e.g., available memory)

This data does **not** include your name, email address, or precise location, and is used solely to identify and fix technical issues.

### 3.3 Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- Your name or email address
- Payment or financial information
- Photos, camera access, or microphone data
- Health or biometric data
- Social media profile information

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Deliver the core features of the App (location-based tourism guidance)
- Diagnose and resolve technical errors and crashes
- Improve the stability, performance, and user experience of the App

We do **not** use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to App functionality.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Location data** is processed in real time to deliver App features and is not stored on our servers.
- **Crash reports** are retained by the crash reporting service for a limited period (typically 90 days) and are used solely for debugging purposes.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right to access** — You may request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to deletion** — You may request that we delete any personal data associated with your use of the App.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — You may revoke location access at any time via your device settings.
- **Right to lodge a complaint** — You may lodge a complaint with a relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [support@meridianguide.com](mailto:support@meridianguide.com).

## 10. International Users

Zanzibar Meridian Guide is available globally. If you are accessing the App from the **European Union**, your use is subject to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). If you are accessing from **California, USA**, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) may apply to you.

In both cases, the rights outlined in Section 9 apply to you, and you may contact us at any time to exercise them.
